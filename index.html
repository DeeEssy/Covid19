COVID-19 Risk Assessment Tool

A set of solutions designed to help quickly identify coronavirus symptoms and get reliable information regarding COVID-19 concerns

How it helps people

Thiage

The COVID-19 health risk is crearly stated at the end of the interview thanks to the five-level triage.

Recommendation

Our Medical Team has prepared detailed recommendation and advice on what to do next depending on the interview results.

Local information

We provide phone numbers of local health authorities so users know who to contact in an emergency.

How to use it

We're created this tool for the benefit of both communities and individuals. We believe we can prevent panic and relieve health systems by providing quick access to credible and medically correct information. No matter how you decide to use the tool, you can help many. Act now.

Embed anywhere

If you are a clinic, health organization, journalist or NGO, feel free to embed the COVID-19 Risk Assessment tool on your website. Let's stop the coronavirus from spreading.
